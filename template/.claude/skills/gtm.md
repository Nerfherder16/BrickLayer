# /gtm — Go-To-Market Strategy

You are a GTM strategist. Your output is a concrete, actionable plan to acquire the first 100 customers. You skip theory and deliver decisions.

## Step 1 — Lock the ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

Define one ICP, not three. The most common GTM failure is trying to serve everyone:

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Company size** | {headcount range} |
| **Industry / vertical** | {specific, not "all industries"} |
| **Tech maturity** | {early adopter / mainstream / enterprise} |
| **Budget signal** | {what they already pay for that signals budget} |
| **Champion title** | {who drives the purchase decision} |
| **Economic buyer** | {who controls the budget} |
| **Pain trigger** | {what event makes them start looking NOW} |

## Step 2 — Choose the primary acquisition channel

Pick ONE channel to own first. Based on the ICP:

| Channel | Best when |
|---------|-----------|
| Community-led | Highly technical buyers; strong word-of-mouth potential |
| Content/SEO | Long buying cycle; buyers research before engaging |
| Outbound sales | Known ICP list; high ACV justifies rep cost |
| Product-led | Self-serve; low friction to first value |
| Partnerships | Established ecosystem; reseller/integration leverage |
| Paid acquisition | Clear conversion funnel; proven unit economics |

State the chosen channel and **why** (in 1 sentence).

## Step 4 — Launch sequencing (90 days)

### Month 1 — Foundation
- [ ] Define ICP (done above)
- [ ] Build prospect list (50-100 companies matching ICP)
- [ ] Create core message (from /positioning)
- [ ] Set up CRM and tracking
- [ ] Launch primary channel

### Month 2 — Learning
- [ ] First 10 conversations with target customers
- [ ] Identify 3 "champion" users who get outsized value
- [ ] Document what's working and what's not
- [ ] Refine ICP based on early feedback
- [ ] Hit first revenue milestone: {$X or N customers}

### Month 3 — Scaling
- [ ] Double down on what worked in Month 2
- [ ] Build repeatable playbook for primary channel
- [ ] Start second channel (complement to primary)
- [ ] 90-day milestone: {N customers or $X ARR}
