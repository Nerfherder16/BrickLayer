Campaign Conclusions

Capacity Is the Hard Ceiling

Employee count is identical across all interest rate scenarios — capacity schedule, not interest rate, determines how many employees can enroll. D9 and D14/D15 don't move headcount; they only move L and TTF.

9% Rate — Recommended Sweet Spot

At 9% escrow interest: L stays ≥35% Healthy zone through entire Phase 1. Phase 2 recovers to 75.5% L in 8 years — full shutdown achievable without hitting 100% during operations.

12% Rate — Fastest Shutdown Path

At 12% escrow interest: L holds ~49% flat through all 20 years (interest income balances enrollment dilution). Phase 2 L crosses 100% at P2 Year 6, enabling full shutdown in just 6 years post-pause.
