## Backward Compatibility

`[\w][\w.-]*` matches any alphanumeric-starting identifier including BL 1.x Q-prefix IDs. No BL 1.x questions are broken.

## Impact on _mark_question_done()

The `"\n## "` search now correctly finds the next block boundary for any BL 2.0 question header. Previously, `next_block` was always -1 for BL 2.0 questions (no `\n## Q` match), causing `block_end = len(text)` and capturing the rest of the file. The replace(count=1) still worked correctly in that case, but the new search is more precise.
