## Finding 3: Sunday cron schedule confirmed in source

`src/workers/main.py` lines 425–431:

```python
# Reconcile Qdrant/Neo4j orphans weekly Sunday 5:30am (after profile drift at 5am)
cron(
    run_reconcile,
    weekday=6,
    hour=5,
    minute=30,
)
```

`weekday=6` = Sunday (arq convention: 0=Monday … 6=Sunday). The cron is scheduled for exactly 2026-03-16T05:30 UTC as the question posits. The cron job is registered in `cron_jobs` alongside all other workers, meaning it will fire when the worker process is alive at that time.
