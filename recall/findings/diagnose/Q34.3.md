Queried `/admin/audit?action=consolidate&limit=100`. Of the 100 returned entries, 33 consolidation events occurred strictly after the repair timestamp (14:17:41 UTC) through ~15:10 UTC, a rate of approximately 34/hour.

Per Q31.5 root cause: `mark_superseded` does not zero Neo4j importance, so each consolidation supersession should produce one new mismatch. Yet the reconcile scans show near-zero mismatches despite active consolidation, which implies either:

(a) The consolidation pattern during this window predominantly produced new memories (create path) rather than supersessions that conflict with an existing Qdrant importance, or
(b) A same-session create-then-supersede cycle resolves within the reconcile polling interval (the transient 1-mismatch at 14:32 suggests (b) — it appeared briefly and self-resolved).
