### HIGH/MEDIUM (fixed — 5 libuv Pattern 3 recurrences)

| `session-save.js` | Recall | `process.exit(0)` → `return`, catch → noop |
| `masonry-pre-compact.js` | Masonry | `process.exit(0)` → removed, catch → noop |
| `masonry-session-summary.js` | Masonry | `process.exit(0)` → removed, catch → noop |

Commits: `10f3c3b` (Masonry), `486dfa6` (Recall/master)

### MEDIUM (fixed)

- Commit: `10f3c3b`
