## Analysis

When all heal cycles complete without producing FIXED, the for-loop at line 165 exhausts its range and falls through to line 332. The exhaustion handler:
1. Appends a note to the original finding file (correct).
2. Logs to stderr (correct).
3. Returns `current_result` (the last FIX_FAILED result, with verdict reset to FAILURE at line 322).

It does not call `update_results_tsv(original_qid, "HEAL_EXHAUSTED", ...)` or any equivalent. The original question's row in `results.tsv` retains the FAILURE (or DIAGNOSIS_COMPLETE) verdict it held before `run_heal_loop()` was called.

This produces permanent staleness: a question that ran 3 full heal cycles and remained unresolved looks identical in results.tsv to a question that just failed with no heal attempt. There is no way to distinguish exhausted-heal from untried-heal by reading results.tsv alone.
