### Scan Logic: writeback_optimized_instructions() — confirmed scan depth

Source: `masonry/scripts/run_optimization.py` lines 144–158.

The function scans exactly three categories of candidate paths:

1. `{base_dir}/.claude/agents/{agent}.md` — project-root level
2. `{base_dir}/{child}/.claude/agents/{agent}.md` for every non-hidden, non-dot directory under `base_dir` — ONE level deep only
3. `~/.claude/agents/{agent}.md` — user-global agents dir

The iteration is `base_dir.iterdir()` with the guard `child.is_dir() and not child.name.startswith(".")`. This is strictly one level deep. Directories two or more levels from `base_dir` (e.g., `projects/adbp2/`, `projects/ADBP3/`, `projects/bl2/`) are never iterated.
