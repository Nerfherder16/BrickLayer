## Clarification: question_text Field Location

The task spec instructs checking `question_text` as a top-level field. In `scored_all.jsonl`, this top-level field is always empty — by design. The enrichment (F29.1) populates `input.question_text`, which is the correct field for DSPy training data matching `ResearchAgentSig`'s `InputField` declaration (`signatures.py` line 15). The `training_extractor.py` reads `record["input"]["question_text"]` at optimization time. The enrichment is architecturally correct; the spec's reference to top-level `question_text` is a field-path ambiguity, not a missing feature.
