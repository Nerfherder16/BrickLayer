## Summary

`_tool_masonry_drift_check()` in `server.py` reads `agent_db.json` from disk at every invocation. There is no module-level cache or import-time loading of the file. The `run_drift_check()` call chains to `agent_db_path.read_text()` inside `drift_detector.py`. After `sync_verdicts_to_agent_db.py` writes updated verdicts, the next `masonry_drift_check` call will see the updated data immediately.
