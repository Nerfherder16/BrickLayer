### Note on apostrophe edge case

The E13.7 spec used `what\s+(is|'s)\s+broken` but `\b` before `'` causes the pattern to fail on `what's` (apostrophe is not a word character). Fixed by splitting into two explicit alternatives: `what\s+is\s+broken|what's\s+broken`.
