**Mode**: evolve

### Root cause of null last_score for 9 trained agents

The `last_score` field is only written to `agent_registry.yml` when `improve_agent.py` or `eval_agent.py` is explicitly run for that agent. Many agents received training records via `score_ops_agents.py` batch scoring but were never individually evaluated. The registry field reflects explicit eval runs, not the existence of training data.
